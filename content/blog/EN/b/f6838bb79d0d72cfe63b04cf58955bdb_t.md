         <i></i>   <i></i>   <i></i> 1<p><img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/10/c-users-zainab-afridi-downloads-20-10-2463-2-jpg.jpeg"/></p> <h2>Bleeding Candles</h2> <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/10/word-image-1436.jpeg"/>Tapoos.com <p>Turn your candles into bleeding votive that really set the tone for an eerie evening of Halloween fun.</p> <p><strong>Materials:</strong></p>  White candles Red candle Nails  <p><strong>Instructions:</strong></p>  Make interesting pierced candles using <strong>nails</strong>.  <p><img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/10/word-image-1437.jpeg"/></p>  Burn the red candle and drip the melted red wax on top of the pierced candle until it runs the length of the candle.  <p><img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/10/word-image-1438.jpeg"/></p>  Arrange the finished candles in a group together for an eye catching display.  <h2>Spider Web</h2> <p>Turn your unused trash bag into a spooky spider web!</p> <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/10/word-image-1439.jpeg"/>Tapoos.com <p><strong>Materials:</strong></p>  Trash bag Tape Marker  <p><strong>Instructions:</strong></p>  Lay a trash bag out flat and trim off the side and bottom seams so you’re left with a big rectangle of plastic. Cut each piece into an approximate square.  <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/10/word-image-1440.jpeg"/>Tapoos.com  Fold the square in half diagonally to form a triangle. Then, fold that triangle in half two more times so that it is narrow.  <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/10/word-image-1441.jpeg"/>Tapoos.com  As it is slippery, use a few pieces of tape to fasten the layers where the loose folded edges come together. Use a visible color marker to draw the shape of a <strong>stem</strong> from the folded point out to the end of your triangle for your web as shown in the image.  <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/10/word-image-1442.jpeg"/>Tapoos.com  Cut out the negative shapes and unfold the web.  <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/10/word-image-1443.jpeg"/>Tapoos.com  Hang the web on your wall to complement the rest of your Halloween decorations.  <h2>Scary Lanterns</h2> <p>Light up your Halloween with these scary lanterns made from hot glue.</p>     <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/10/word-image-1444.jpeg"/>Tapoos.com <p><strong>Materials:</strong></p>  Carbon paper White glue Tall drinking glass Hot glue Matte finish paint (own color choice) Chalk Fairy lights      <p><strong>Instructions:</strong></p>  Draw eyes and mouth on the carbon paper with a marker. Apply some white glue on the back of the paper and stick it on the glass. Now make the outline of the face using hot glue and put the hot glue around the rim of the glass imitating dripping melted wax.  <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/10/word-image-1445.jpeg"/>Tapoos.com  Once dry, start painting the glass with the black matte finish paint and avoid painting the eyes and mouth. Then, let it dry.  <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/10/word-image-1446.jpeg"/>Tapoos.com  Put chalk on each drop and smear it to give it a 3D effect  <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/10/c-users-zainab-afridi-downloads-20-10-2463-13-jpg.jpeg"/>Tapoos.com  And you’re done. Put some lights or candle inside the glass and let it illuminate the spooky night.    <i></i>
