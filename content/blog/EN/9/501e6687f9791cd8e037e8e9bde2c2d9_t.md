         <i></i>   <i></i>   <i></i> 1<p><img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/12/c-users-mahad-downloads-4-12-3060-jpg.jpeg"/></p> <p>What is that first thing which comes to your mind when you hear that concept of “Happy Married Life”? Well, if you follow the fantasy world a lot, watch too much movies, or read too many books, then you must have the idea of Perfect happy marriage as something all about flowers, gifts, candle light dinner dates, and diamonds. But in this life of the real world, there is no such fictitious or material thing that will make a marriage happy. In fact, these can just act as a cherry to the top on the cake of happily married life.</p> <p>Now the questions arises that if these all cannot make a marriage happy, then what is it that will make your married life perfect? Well the answer is simple that if it is not diamonds and flowers that make a marriage happy, then there are the little things and some small, simple steps over time that will yield bigger and happier results for your married life. This is very oft repeated fact that married life takes some effort to maintain a charm in it every day, and even after years. All this effort is mainly about doing something cute and delighting for your partner every single day to build a meaningful and lasting relationship. But it does not necessarily means that you have to work very hard work.</p> <p>Here below we are compiling some very small and simple things you can easily do to make your life with your partner worth living every day.</p> <h2>14.</h2> <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/12/word-image-34.jpeg"/>Tapoos <p>As you move forward from you honey moon period, spending time with your partner becomes just a daily routine matter for you, you will soon notice that it is becoming easy for you to find things about your partner that are exasperating, irritating or sometimes even hurtful to you. Well this is that time when, as a spouse, one of your principal duties is to be positive and stop criticizing your partner and staring to focus on the positive things that they do.</p> <h2>13.</h2> <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/12/word-image-35.jpeg"/>Tapoos <p>There will come times for you in a married life when you will feel that you are very right and your partner is being very wrong. Well, what you’re thinking might or might not be true. But always remember that each and every time the couples fight, they are likely to criticize and blame each other instead of taking things in a rational way, and this leads to a lot of other problems. In order to lead a happy married life, you should make a personal commitment to yourself that you will use no blame in your communication and will always be rational and think about things with your mind instead of being over emotional. Every time you are explaining things to your spouse think about your feelings and needs and speak in terms of ‘I.’ instead of “you”.</p> <h2>12.</h2> <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/12/word-image-36.jpeg"/>Tapoos <p>There should be no such thing as a feeling of superiority or ego between couples if they really want to be happy. If you realize that you have done something wrong, no matter whether it is big or small, it is your duty to bite the bullet and give a clear and honest apology to your partner. Do not ever feel shy of be reluctant while apologizing to your spouse. Remember that apologizing won’t ever make you small in the eyes of your partner, in fact this apologizing will raise your respect and value in the eyes of your partner. Apologizing for your mistakes means that the health of your relationship is more important to you over anything and that is what Love actually means.</p> <h2>11.</h2> <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/12/word-image-37.jpeg"/>Tapoos <p>Ladies! Never ever ask your partner to change his beard style of to have a better wardrobe. Same goes for men, never complain about your wife’s increasing weight or her freckles. If you do so, this will really hurt your partner and will also weaken the string that is holding you two together. Accepting your spouse with his or her flaws is what true love is.</p>     <h2>10.</h2> <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/12/word-image-38.jpeg"/>Tapoos <p>Showing appreciation goes a long way to help build a good connection with your partner. If you try to look a little more deeply, you will see that there are a hell lot of things that your partner does which are cute and makes your heart skip a beat. You can also find out many things about your partner which he or she outshines in. Try to find out as many such things as possible and let your partner know that your appreciate them.</p> <h2>9.</h2> <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/12/word-image-39.jpeg"/>Tapoos <p>You are not really into sports but your husband loves football? Or you don’t like watching romantic movies, but your wife wants a good snack with a romantic movie every Saturday night? Then the best option for you in this scenario is trying your best to make their hobbies yours and getting interested in the things your partner loves. In this way, you will gradually be sharing hobbies and will be able to have more fun filled time together, which is a secret to a prefect married life.</p> <h2>4.</h2> <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/12/word-image-40.jpeg"/>Tapoos <p>In the modern world, people are so busy with their gadgets that they don’t have enough time to sit and talk. Well this is going to sound a little Filmy, but it is advisable for a couple to leave the technology and every other distraction for some time each week and just sit in cozy environment under a starry sky and talk to each other. No matter what is it that you talk about, just remain attentive to your partner and listen to him or her. Showing interest in what your partner says will actually make them feel important and this will make your importance in their eyes higher too.</p> <h2>3.</h2> <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/12/word-image-41.jpeg"/>Tapoos <p>This is literally everything your partner will ever ask you for. They may never say it openly but as they say some things are best when left unsaid, do not ever push them to the limit where they are forced to say it themselves. Do it yourself. No matter whether you are at a friend’s get-together, a business meeting, or are just walking down the street, give him or her subtle signals of your connection. Give importance to them; do not ever ignore them for anyone and never, ever, make fun of your partner in public or try to humiliate him or her even if you do not mean it and are just trying to look entertaining.</p> <h2>2.</h2> <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/12/word-image-42.jpeg"/>Tapoos <p>Even if your partner is never asking for it, try to work on yourself a little more so that you can be better towards your family.</p> <h2>1.</h2> <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/12/word-image-43.jpeg"/>Tapoos     <p>Well, it is never old fashioned to go for long drives and long walks. Doing such cute things on and off, removes small misunderstandings between two partners and also strengthen the bond of love between them.</p>   <i></i>
