         <i></i>   <i></i>   <i></i> 0<p>The Uncomfortable is a list of household objects that are too impractical. Behind these objects is Katerina Kamprani who is based in Athens. The work is done on common kitchen items like dishware, watering cans, cutlery etc. and it is designed in such a way that it becomes impossible or very difficult to use. The material used is the same used by the object but it’s given a humorous touch to it.</p> <p>Before making the physical object, Katerina makes a 3D model so that she tests its shape. <strong>Tapoos </strong>has gathered 40 images of the work done by the talented artist.
