19 Times People Had One Job and Failed Dramatically

     <i></i>   <i></i>   <i></i> 1<p> </p> <p>There are people who in spite of being given easy jobs, manage to mess up trivial details of their work. From basic road design to labelling things wrong, these small details tend to drive OCD driven people absolutely insane. Here are some instances of people screwing up the most mundane of jobs, creating grievances for everyone who sets their eyes upon the problem.</p> <h2>19. This misguided designer</h2> <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/10/word-image-1648.jpeg"/>Imgur <p>There is something to be said about overworked designers. This poor designer was so overwhelmed at work that he couldn’t make sense of normal colors. On a pastel pink shirt, he can be seen advertising the color green as the new black. Well to that we would just like to say, what a nice blue shirt!</p> <h2>18. This obviously shy traffic signal</h2> <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/10/word-image-1649.jpeg"/>Imgur <p>Some things are so pointless in their existence and lack of utility, that it would not make a difference if they were to exist or not. Such is the nature of this traffic signal. This shy traffic light is facing away from the roads, instead of towards the traffic. This means that oncoming traffic can’t tell when to come and when to stop their engines, making this traffic light as good as useless. It is safe to say that it would have made no difference if they were to remove the traffic light altogether. However, imagine all the traffic congestions this shy little bugger has led to cause!</p> <h2>17. The mis-constructed Pikachu</h2> <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/10/word-image-1650.jpeg"/>Imgur <p>Most of us have grown up obsessing over anime, specifically Pokemon. Everyone from Ash to Misty were all little kids and teens could talk about when the show was initially screening on television. Pokemon merchandise production was and still is on an all-time high. From Pokemon cards to action figurines, Pokemon aficionados collect everything. A hot favorite Pokemon was Ash’s very own sidekick, Pikachu. This particular picture of him shows a disfigured action figure with its tail attached on top of its head and it’s ears coming out of it’s rear. It is both annoying and funny to see a childhood favorite this way!</p> <h2>16. The shoddy car job</h2> <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/10/word-image-1651.jpeg"/>Imgur <p>Some people take car maintenance very seriously while others decide to just wing it however they can. This particular car got a makeover it clearly did not need. While the car, with the rusty paint and the shoddy tyres, was in a questionable condition from the start, the paint job did nothing to improve its outlook. A mere streak of white splattered all over the hood of the car, the paint job makes it look like a man accidentally swiped a coat of paint on the hood and then decided to quit midway, without thinking of reverting his mistake. It definitely looks like a case of a badly going pimp my ride episode!</p> <h2>15. One eyed Anna</h2> <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/10/word-image-1652.jpeg"/>Imgur <p>For most of us, Frozen stood on the shoulders of Anna and Olaf, the film’s most lovable characters by far. Seeing our favorite character reduced to being a creepy lopsided princess is enough to make any one of fume with anger. This version of Anna has two starkly different eyeballs popping out from each of her sockets making her seem like something out of the Terminator franchise. All we can say to the person who is behind designing this atrocity is that if they ever even come near designing something as sinister as this is, “We’ll be back’. *cue terminator music*</p> <h2>14. This helpful sign</h2> <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/10/word-image-1653.jpeg"/>Imgur <p>In case of fire, take a hold of the nearest staircase and start hitting the fire until it submits to your will and subsides itself. This fire warning sign depicts a man holding what looks like a staircase and he can be seen, what can only be described as, hitting the fire.
