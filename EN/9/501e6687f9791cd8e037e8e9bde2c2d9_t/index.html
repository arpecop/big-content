Well the answer is simple that if it is not diamonds and flowers that make a marriage happy, then there are the little things and some small, simple steps over time that will yield bigger and happier results for your married life. This is very oft repeated fact that married life takes some effort to maintain a charm in it every day, and even after years. Well, what you’re thinking might or might not be true. But always remember that each and every time the couples fight, they are likely to criticize and blame each other instead of taking things in a rational way, and this leads to a lot of other problems. In order to lead a happy married life, you should make a personal commitment to yourself that you will use no blame in your communication and will always be rational and think about things with your mind instead of being over emotional. Every time you are explaining things to your spouse think about your feelings and needs and speak in terms of ‘I.’ instead of “you”.</p> <h2>12.</h2> <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/12/word-image-36.jpeg"/>Tapoos <p>There should be no such thing as a feeling of superiority or ego between couples if they really want to be happy. Do not ever feel shy of be reluctant while apologizing to your spouse. Remember that apologizing won’t ever make you small in the eyes of your partner, in fact this apologizing will raise your respect and value in the eyes of your partner. Apologizing for your mistakes means that the health of your relationship is more important to you over anything and that is what Love actually means.</p> <h2>11.</h2> <img src="https://cdnone.netlify.com/db/2017/12/word-image-37.jpeg"/>Tapoos <p>Ladies!
